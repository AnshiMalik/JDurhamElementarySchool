Campus Life

School Multi-purpose Room

The multi-purpose room is used for special ocasions, physical education, monthly assembly, and is also used as a cafeteria!



The School Library

The school library has many books for all the different grade levels, and many interesting series. You can check out 3 books each week, and meet the sweet librarian!



The Playground

The playground has many fun features which makes it enjoyable! There are 3 different playgrounds in the campus!

Ranking

J. Haley Durham Elementary School is ranked within the top 10% of all 9,659 schools in California (based off of combined math and reading proficiency testing data) for the 2018-19 school year.

Awards

Durham Elementary received both state and national recognition as a California Distinguished School 2012, a National Blue Ribbon School 2012, a California Title I Academic Achievement Award recipient in 2013 and 2016, a Campaign for Business and Education Excellence Honor Roll recipient 2014, 2015 and 2016, a Campaign for Business and Education Excellence Honor Roll STEM recipient 2015 (for Math and Science), a CA Gold Ribbon School 2016, and an Innovate Top Bay Area Public School Leading in Educational Equity 2017 and 2019.

Beating the State Average

Durham Elementary is a great school as it beats California's average percentage in mathematics, reading, and language arts
